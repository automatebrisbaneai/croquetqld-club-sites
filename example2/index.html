Four Lawns on the Ridge

Association, Golf, Ricochet and Social croquet on the Sunshine Coast. Six days a week, all year round.

Come and Try

More Than a Game

Four lawns in the leafiest corner of the Sunshine Coast. Some play for competition: teams match up across the region, and the best get picked for State and national squads. Others come for the morning air, a bit of exercise, and the company.

Both belong here.

We play Association, Golf Croquet, Ricochet and Social six days a week. The lawns are open, the kettle is on, and there is always someone happy to show you the ropes.
