Come and Try

More Than a Game

Four lawns in the leafiest corner of the Sunshine Coast. Some play for competition: teams match up across the region, and the best are selected for State and national squads. Others come for the morning air, a little exercise, and the company.

Both belong here.

We play Association, Golf Croquet, Ricochet and Social six days a week. The lawns are open, the kettle is on, and there is always someone glad to show you how it works.
