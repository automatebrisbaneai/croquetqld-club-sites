When we play

All sessions are open to beginners. Golf Croquet (GC) on Sunday, Monday, Tuesday and Friday is the easiest place to start. Ricochet (RC) and Association (AC) are also welcoming to newcomers.

New player? Just turn up at 2:30 pm any weekday or Sunday, ask for the secretary (Pauline), and we'll take it from there. No booking needed.

Who we are

Est. 1970. Three lawns. One community.

Home to three current Queensland state representatives and host of the 2023 QLD State Titles. Three lawns in the quiet streets of Chelmer, ten minutes from the city.

Our players include state-level competitors and people who picked up a mallet for the first time last month. We have coaching, competitive ladders, and coffee after most sessions.

First session is free. All equipment supplied. Just bring flat shoes and a hat.
