Who we are

Brisbane's game.

Three lawns in the quiet streets of Chelmer, ten minutes from the city. Graceville has been part of this community since 1970. Some play for competition, others for the company. Both belong here.

Our players include state-level competitors and people who picked up a mallet for the first time last month. We have coaching, we have competitive ladders, and we have coffee after most sessions.

First session is free. All equipment supplied. Just bring flat shoes and a hat.
