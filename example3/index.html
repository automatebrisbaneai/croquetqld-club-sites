About the club

Serious play. Good company.

Some come for competition. Teams match up across the region, and the best get picked for State and national squads. Others come for the morning air, a bit of exercise, and the company. Both belong here.

Four full-size lawns on the ridge at Buderim. Open six days a week. Association, Golf Croquet, Ricochet, and Social Croquet.

  • First session completely free. No obligation.
  • Bring flat shoes and a hat. All equipment supplied.
  • No experience needed. No booking required.
  • Coaching available from $15 per session.
  • Just turn up to any listed session.
