About the club

Serious play. Good company.

Some come for competition. Teams match up across the region, and the best get picked for State and national squads. Others come for the morning air, a bit of exercise, and the company. Both belong here.

Four lawns on the ridge, in Buderim. Open six days a week. Association, Golf, Ricochet, and Social Croquet.

New players

Your first session is free.

Bring flat shoes and a hat. We supply everything else. No experience needed, no booking required. Turn up to any session.

  • All equipment supplied (mallets and balls)
  • First session completely free
  • Coaching available: $15 per session
  • Golf and Association Croquet coaching with qualified coaches
  • Just show up to any session listed above
