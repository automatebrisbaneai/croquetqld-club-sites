About the Club

More Than a Game

The Ipswich Croquet Club has been part of Queens Park for many years. We are a friendly, sociable club, and Tuesday mornings are as much about the morning tea as anything that happens on the lawn.

"Kevin has been president for twelve years and knows everyone by name. We're not serious competitors, we just love the game and love getting together."

Younger players are coming through now too. If you've always been curious about croquet, this is a good place to start.

New to Croquet

Come and Try

Every first Saturday of the month we run a Come and Try morning. No equipment needed, no experience expected. Just show up at Queens Park off Ellenborough Street and follow the signs to the lawns.

A club member will walk you through the basics. You'll play a few ends, have a chat over morning tea, and see if it suits you. Most people who come once come back.

Stay for morning tea. It's part of the tradition.

Getting Here

Our lawns are in Queens Park, Ipswich. The main entrance to the croquet grounds is off Ellenborough Street. There is a car park just inside the gate. If you're coming for the first time, park near the gate and walk toward the lawns. You'll hear us before you see us.
