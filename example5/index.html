How to Come and Play View the Schedule
New Players Welcome

Come and try for free

Your first session costs nothing. No experience, no special gear. Pick a session and turn up.

No booking needed

Just arrive at any session. Someone will welcome you, explain the game, and get you on a lawn within a few minutes.

Everything provided

Mallets, balls, and hoops are supplied. Bring flat-soled shoes and a hat. That is all you need.

Coaching available

Qualified coaches run $15 sessions in Golf and Association Croquet. Ask about coaching when you arrive.
