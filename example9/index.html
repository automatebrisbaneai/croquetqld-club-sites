01 / About

A club that knows
what it's about.

Four lawns on the ridge, in the leafiest corner of the Sunshine Coast. Some play for competition, teams match up across the region, and the best get picked for State and national squads.

Others come for the morning air, a bit of exercise, and the company. Both belong here.

Six days a week. All year. Closed Thursday.
