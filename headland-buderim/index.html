About the Club

More Than a Game on the Ridge

Headland Buderim sits 100 metres above the coast in one of the Sunshine Coast's leafiest corners. Four full-size courts, more than fifty members, and sessions running six days a week across three codes.

Some play for competition — our teams match up across the region, and our best get picked for State and national squads. Others come for the morning air, a bit of exercise, and the company. Both belong here.

Every session has a social break. The coaches are qualified. The clubhouse is warm. The lawns are good. That's about it, really.

Getting Started

Never Held a Mallet?

No worries. Turn up to any session listed above. Your first go is free, and we supply everything — mallet, balls, the lot. Just bring flat shoes and a hat.

Your first session is completely free. No booking. No commitment. Just show up, introduce yourself, and we'll sort you out.
