About the Club

More Than a Game

Headland Buderim is one of Queensland's larger croquet clubs. We have four full-size courts, more than fifty members, and sessions running six days a week across three codes: Association Croquet, Golf Croquet, and Ricochet.

Some of us play for the competition — our teams go up against clubs across the Sunshine Coast region, and our best players get picked for State and national squads. Others just want fresh air, a bit of exercise, and good company on a Tuesday morning. Both are welcome here.

Every session includes a social break. We run events through the year. Our coaches are fully qualified and work with beginners through to advanced players. The clubhouse is available for hire, and we run group introduction sessions for workplaces and community groups.

Getting Started

Come & Try

Never held a mallet? No worries. Turn up to any session — your first go is free, and we supply all the gear. Flat shoes and a hat are all you need to bring.

Your first session is free. No booking required. Just come along to any session listed above and introduce yourself. We'll sort you out with a mallet and show you the ropes.

If you want a proper introduction, our qualified coaches run beginner sessions for $15. They'll cover the basics of both Golf Croquet and Association Croquet, so you can find which one suits you.

Interested in a group session for your workplace, school, or community group? We run those too — get in touch with Sherry Tusler to arrange one.
