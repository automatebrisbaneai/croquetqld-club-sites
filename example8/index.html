About the Club

Community, Friendly, Bundaberg

The Bundaberg Croquet Club has been part of life along the Burnett for many years. We are a community club in the truest sense, and we welcome anyone who wants to give the game a go. Our members range from seasoned competitors to first-timers, and our champion player Terry holds three Queensland state titles. We're proud of our competition record and prouder still of the club atmosphere.
