About the Club

Part of Toowoomba since the 1940s.

We were founded in 1941 and have been in Queens Park ever since. Most of our members have been coming for years, some for decades. We get a good mix: competitive players on Tuesdays and Fridays, and the social crowd on Tuesday and Friday afternoons. The club plays four forms of croquet across four full-size lawns, six days a week.

A gentle sport with more depth than it looks. You'll be back the following Tuesday.

Winter (May to August): social sessions shift to 2 pm. All other times remain the same.

What are the codes? Golf Croquet is a simpler game and the best place to start. Turns alternate; first to six hoops wins. Association Croquet is the traditional game with more strategy and longer play. Ricochet is a four-ball variation. Social sessions are relaxed and mixed. All sessions are played on our four full-size lawns behind the pavilion.
Visiting from another club: Tuesday 9 am Golf Croquet is our main competitive session. Play is at a good standard. Affiliated visitors are welcome without prior arrangement. Any questions before you drive out, call Margaret on 0413 544 309.
