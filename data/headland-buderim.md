# Headland-Buderim Croquet Club

Source: https://www.croquetqld.org/club-information/headland-buderim/

## Basic Info

- **Club name:** Headland Buderim Croquet Club
- **Location:** 3 Orme Road, Buderim QLD 4556
- **Region:** Sunshine Coast
- **Members:** ~50
- **Courts:** 4 full-size lawns
- **Codes played:** Association, Golf, Ricochet, Social
- **Open:** 6 days a week (closed Thursday)

## About

Four lawns on the ridge, in the leafiest corner of the Sunshine Coast. Some play for competition, teams match up across the region, and the best get picked for State and national squads. Others come for the morning air, a bit of exercise, and the company. Both belong here.

## Come & Try

- First session is free
- All equipment supplied (mallets, balls)
- $15 coaching sessions with qualified coaches covering Golf and Association Croquet
- Just bring flat shoes and a hat
- Turn up to any session listed above

## Fees

- Come & Try (first session): FREE
- Visitor (affiliated club): $10
- Visitor (other): $15
- Beginner coaching: $15
- Green fees (member daily): $8
